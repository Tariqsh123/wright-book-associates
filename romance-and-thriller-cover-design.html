Our 6 Step Process

1

First Contact

At Wright Book Associate, to kickstart your journey toward your book cover design, simply visit our website and complete a brief form. Share all the necessary details (Don’t miss out on anything.) Once you have done it, after reviewing your application, a member of our responsive customer service team will promptly get in touch with you.

2

Research and Discussion

After going over your form, we’ll connect you to one of our book cover artists, best suited to your story's needs, and bring your vision to the cover. They'll research all your needs and present you with their plans and design strategies. In these discussions, you'll have all the liberty to approve and disapprove ideas if they do not align with your vision.

3

Draft Approval

Now, keeping in mind your book's genre, plot, characters, and your and your audience's interests, our professional book cover designers will be preparing a first draft of your book cover. They'll also be combining their experience and expertise to carefully design the perfect custom book cover for you. Then, we'll hand it over for you to approve.

4

Detailing

Once your draft is meticulously refined and customised to meet your requirements, we'll delve into the finer details. We'll work closely with you to ensure that the colour scheme, fonts, and any additional icons are not only in sync with your preferences but also elevate the quality of your book cover design to match your vision.

5

Final Formatting

After it’s been edited, it’ll be sent over to a separate team of our professional book cover designers to make sure everything is perfect. They'll go over it a few more times and make edits wherever needed, whether it's the colour, fonts, or illustrations themselves, just to make sure that your book cover design is perfect in every detail.

6

Approval and Closing

Now, our team of professional book designers will be sending the book cover design your way once it’s gone through the final stage of editing and proofing, just so you can approve the final masterpiece. With our professional book cover design services, our designers offer an unlimited number of revisions just so you're completely on board with the final piece.
